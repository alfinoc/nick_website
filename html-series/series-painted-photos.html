NA//Painted Photos

Painted Photos

Painted Photos is a collaborative, multimedia project of photos used as painting inspiration by artist, Kat Gu. The photo inspirations were selected by Kat out of large collections of film and digital photos from travel in Benin, Italy, China from ’11-’16.

Using enamel, acrylic and oil on paper, the paintings experiment with small scale work on paper, completed quickly. As an experimentation with fluid materials and ways of applying paint by pouring, mixing, dripping, and collaging, the abstract conception transforms form and shape from the conceived photo.

Completed between fall ’16 and jan ’17, the work completes a notion of transposing quick glances of visual reality into small, ephemeral works on paper.
